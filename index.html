Started watching the new Fallout series from Amazon and so far it's pretty damn good. So good that it got me playing Fallout 4 again, which as I play again I honestly ekeep closing it out. Maybe I just like the story of Fallout but kind of just lose interest because of the gameplay.

One thing that I believe that is making me come to terms of just liking the story of Fallout, is probably the fact that Bethesda (the company responsible for the Elder Scrolls and Fallout series), tends to release games that have a TON of bugs at the beginning of their life and it's getting annoying. I solely blame their fans for letting them get away with releasing unfinished projects. Hopefully Elder Scrolls VI isn't like that.

I believe as a collective, we shouldn't allow these "super-rich" creators publish work that is mediocre at launch, especially if they have the funding to not do so. GO AHEAD! Push back your release date and stop saying you have a better version of what you put out already on it's way you money hungry little pig. LOOKING AT YOU SNYDER.
